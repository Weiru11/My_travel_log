Activities

Walking through the city

Sydney felt like a place made for walking. I moved between the harbour, the streets, and the little corners of the city, collecting small views along the way. The best part was not always a specific destination, but the feeling of wandering and slowly understanding the rhythm of the city.

Food

Little meals between plans

Food in Sydney became part of the trip’s memory. Some meals were planned, and some were found by accident while walking around. Whether it was a cafe, a quick bite, or something shared after a long day, these small food moments made the city feel more personal.

Views

Harbour light and open sky

The views were what stayed with me most. The harbour, the water, the buildings, and the sky all seemed to change throughout the day. Sydney was bright and open, but also soft in quiet moments, especially when the light began to shift.
